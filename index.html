Why It Helps

Designed to reduce panic during dictation-style listening.

Focused batches, visible timing, repetition controls, and difficulty filtering keep the task simple when the words get tricky.

Practice Flow

Listen, type, review, repeat.

Use smaller batches to build confidence or larger ones to simulate exam pressure without repeating the same words too soon.

Independent Tool

Built for learning, not official test content.

Use it as a personal companion for spelling accuracy and listening confidence alongside your broader exam preparation.
